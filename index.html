I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, University of Auckland. My research interests lie in the intersection between natural language processing and question answering. Recently my research mainly focuses on machine reasoning. My main research activities involve natural language understanding; topics of interest include problem decomposition, knowledge acquisition, explanation, entailment and generate QA. I am also interested in the graph-based neural network.

I have got a Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Computer Science (First Class) at the University of Auckland recently. During this year, I have completed a Summer Research Internship with Scholarship in Orion Health. Besides, I am one of 10 students who took part in the Summer Research Program funded by Precision Driven Health. The main topic is about Medical Chatbot based on Deep Learning and Knowledge Graph. Besides, I have finished my Dissertation. My research area is the Question Answering Mechanism and Natural Language Processing.

Before I entered the University of Auckland, I finished a double Bachelor degree in Computer Science & Computer and Information Science in China Jiliang University and Auckland University of Technology.

My project background includes a one-year part-time experience at Java Software Development in Service Outsourcing Laboratory of China Jiliang University, one-year part-time experience ACM programmer in ACM program Design Laboratory of China Jiliang University, and two years Mathematics Modelling Training Experience in China Jiliang University.
